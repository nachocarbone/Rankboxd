Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Rankboxd ("we", "our", "the site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data we collect

Rankboxd does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All data is stored locally on your device using localStorage:

  • Your ranking history
  • Language preference (ES/EN)

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time using the "Clear all" button in History.

Third-party services

TMDB (The Movie Database): We use the TMDB API to fetch movie posters. Your Letterboxd file is processed on our server only to search for posters — we do not store it.

Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts to load fonts. Google may collect usage data as per their own privacy policy.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. We only use localStorage for saving your preferences locally.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at @lannisternacho on X.
